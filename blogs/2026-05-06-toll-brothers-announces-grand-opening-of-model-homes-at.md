Last updated on May 7th, 2026 IRVINE, Calif., May 06, 2026 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) — Toll Brothers, Inc. (NYSE:TOL), the nation’s leading builder of luxury homes, today announced the grand opening of 13 beautifully decorated model homes at Toll Brothers at Great Park Neighborhoods, located in Irvine, California. Home shoppers are invited to tour the model homes spanning four distinct collections during the Model Grand Opening event to be held on Saturday, May 16, from 11 a.m. to 2 p.m. at 173 Beverly in Irvine.
