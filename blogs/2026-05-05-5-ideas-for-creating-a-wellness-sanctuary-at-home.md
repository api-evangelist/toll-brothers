Wellness is a popular trend in modern interior home design. Homeowners are increasingly looking for ways to bring balance, well-being, relaxation, and intention into their everyday spaces. Creating a wellness space that nourishes mind, body, and spirit begins with thoughtful design choices that can transform a flex room, loft, or even a quiet corner into a personalized retreat. Whether you’re envisioning a meditation nook, an at-home yoga or fitness studio, or a spa-inspired bathroom retreat, the right design can elevate everyday life and promote well-being.
