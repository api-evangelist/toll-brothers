LAKEWOOD RANCH, Fla., May 07, 2026 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) — Toll Brothers, Inc. (NYSE:TOL), the nation’s leading builder of luxury homes, today announced its newest community, Monterey at Lakewood Ranch – Egret Collection, is coming soon to Lakewood Ranch, Florida. Scheduled to open in summer 2026, this highly anticipated community will feature elegant single-family homes on oversized 90-foot-wide home sites and an exceptional array of amenities.
