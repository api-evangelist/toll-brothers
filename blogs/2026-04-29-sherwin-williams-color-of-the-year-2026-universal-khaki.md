<p>Neutral colors have long been a cornerstone of home design, offering a versatile foundation that allows your personal style to shine through furnishings, textures, and accents. In modern luxury interiors, neutrals continue to evolve, bringing warmth and depth to every space.</p>



<p><a href="https://www.sherwin-williams.com/en-us/color/color-of-the-year/2026" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">Universal Khaki SW 6150</a>, the Sherwin-Williams Color of the Year 2026, reflects this shift toward grounded, timeless design. This warm, earthy neutral features a mid-tone tan base with subtle yellow undertones, striking a balance between comfort and sophistication. Described as “Tailored &amp; Timeless,” Universal Khaki offers both livability and longevity in today’s homes.</p>



<p>Discover how to use Universal Khaki in your home, including the best colors to pair with it, and how <a href="https://www.tollbrothers.com/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">Toll Brothers</a> model homes across the country are incorporating this trending neutral.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">What is Universal Khaki?</h2>



<p>Universal Khaki, a mid-tone neutral from the Sherwin-Williams Classic/Complex palette in their latest <em>Colormix Forecast: Anthology Volume Two</em>, reflects a renewed appreciation for grounded, enduring color in today’s home design. With a warm tan base and subtle undertones, it creates a refined, balanced backdrop that makes intimate rooms and open-concept spaces feel polished and inviting.</p>
